MAX Registration

The Max Registration System (MAXreg) is a product of the students and staff of the .Net Boot Camps of MAX Technical Training.

Four students of the .Net Boot Camp 5 class offered to begin work on the project at the direction of their instructor. There were only 4 business days to work on the project before their DEMO day.
